About

Khándroma is a groove-oriented, instrumental band from Freiburg, Germany. The band‘s music blends elements from jazz, progressive rock, funk and metal, creating a unique sound and musical style, perhaps best described as post-jazz/post-progressive.

Since its formation in 2007, the band‘s core lineup has consisted of piano, electric bass, and drums, with various other instruments such as electric guitar, vocals, synthesizer, percussion, and French horn having been included in past lineups. The band's current lineup features piano, saxophone, electric bass, and drums.

Khándroma can be heard live on a regular basis in their own rehearsal and concert space in Freiburg. Watch out for upcoming show dates.

Khándroma has released four studio albums so far, which are all stylisticly very different, but still represent different facets of the chracteristic Khándroma sound. The most recent album – Chrystal Math – Check out the complete music catalogue.

Musicians

  • Tilman Vogel – Piano
  • Johannes Bernet – Saxophone
  • Philipp Janz - Electric Bass
  • Moriz Ulrich - Drums
