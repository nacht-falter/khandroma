About

Khándroma is a groove-oriented, instrumental band from Freiburg, Germany. The band‘s music blends elements from jazz, progressive rock, funk and metal, creating a unique sound and musical style, perhaps best described as post-jazz/post-progressive.

The band‘s current line-up consists of piano, saxophone, electric bass and drums.play regularly.

Khándroma can be heard live on a regular basis in their own rehearsal and concert space in Freiburg. Watch out for upcoming show dates.

Khándroma has released four studio albums so far, which are all stylisticly very different, but still represent different facets of the chracteristic Khándroma sound. Check out the complete music catalogue.

The Band

  • Tilman Vogel – Piano
  • Johannes Bernet – Saxophone
  • Philipp Janz - Electric Bass
  • Moriz Ulrich - Drums
